Here, I like to share my thoughts on productivity, technology, personal development, and creativity.

- My passion is **music**, specifically **barbershop harmony**. I'm a member of [**Limelight**](https://limelightquartet.co.uk/) quartet, musical director of&nbsp;[**Main Street Sound**](https://mainstreetsound.co.uk), and a founder member of [**York City Harmonisers**](https://yorkcityharmonisers.com).

- I work as a **software engineer** at [**Qured**](https://qured.com). I've worked remotely since 2020, and I specialise in building mobile apps using the [Flutter](https://flutter.dev) framework.

- I've always been obsessed with **software and hardware**. The first computer I used was my grandpa’s desktop PC, which ran Windows 95. Since then I've always been curious about the possibilities that technology can unlock.

- My happy place is **the great outdoors**. I'm fortunate enough to live close to some of Britain's most beautiful countryside, and I love surrounding myself with nature.

- I love **photography** , even though I have **red-green colourblindness**. I try to embrace my vision differences in my photos.

- I have **ADHD** and **Crohn's disease**. One causes brain farts; the other causes real farts. (Sorry.)

- Things that make me happy include **coffee, video games, whisky, good beer, vinyl records, books, British steam trains** , and the **Oxford comma**.

## What to expect from this site

This site is my home on the internet. It's my way of documenting my experiences, work, and interests.

What I choose to post will vary. I might have read an interesting book or blog post, or heard a good podcast. I might have been to a beautiful place, or found an amazing piece of software I think you should try. Sometimes my brain even generates creative ideas of its own that I like to share.

Thanks for stopping by!
