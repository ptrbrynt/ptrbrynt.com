date: 2025-12-04

---

So Spotify Wrapped (and Apple Music Replay etc) is fun an all, but maybe we shouldn’t make this the only time in the whole year to share music with our friends?

Possible 2026 resolution idea: when you hear a new album/artist/song, text a friend about it and see what they think. Have a conversation. Make a connection.
