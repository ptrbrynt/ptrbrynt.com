I’ve just moved my blog from [Ghost](https://ghost.org)&nbsp;to [Pika](https://pika.page). I have no problem with Ghost; I think it’s an amazing platform with a great philosophy, and it’s taking the web in a good direction. I’m rooting for them.

That said, I’m not interested in turning my blog into a business, and the publishing tools provided by Ghost just aren’t for me. I recently discovered Pika, which is half the cost and much simpler. I also prefer the available themes, which support custom CSS without a higher-tier subscription plan.

If, like me, you believe in personal blogging and want a little home on the internet, [Pika](https://pika.page) is a great option.
