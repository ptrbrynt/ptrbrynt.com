A short post about a magical app called [Endel](https://endel.io/).

If you struggle with concentration, boredom, procrastination, or anxiety, you should try Endel. It seems to scratch an itch in my ADHD brain and really helps my executive function.

In particular, I love the Autoplay function which syncs with my circadian rhythm, and also takes into account movement data from my Apple Watch, to provide a soundtrack to match my energy levels throughout the day.

Give it a shot - I love it.
